How to Use ImageIguana

  1. Upload Your Image 🖼️:

    Click that shiny "Upload" button, select your image, and get ready to watch magic happen. Or mild changes. Either way, you're in for a good time.

  2. Select Your Transformation 🌀:

    Do you want your image to be grayscale because you’re feeling artsy? Or maybe you want it in WEBP format because you like living on the edge of technology? Choose wisely, or randomly – we’re not judging.

  3. Click Submit Like You Mean It 🖱️:

    This is the big moment! Click "Submit" and let ImageIguana do the heavy lifting (but let's be honest, it's just a file conversion – we’re not curing world hunger here).

  4. Marvel at Your Masterpiece 🖌️:

    You did it. You uploaded, you selected, you submitted. Now admire your converted image like it's a museum-worthy piece of art. Call your mom and tell her you're a digital artist now.

FAQs (Frequently Asked Quips)

Q: What if I upload a bad photo?
A: ImageIguana will still work, but it might judge you a little. Don’t worry, it’s an iguana, its standards aren’t high.

Q: Why did my photo turn into a gray rectangle?
A: Ah, you chose "Convert to Grayscale." Artistic move. Or did you do it by accident? Either way, it's beautiful. Own it.
